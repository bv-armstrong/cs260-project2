Instructions:

  • 2 of clubs starts.
  • Play proceeds clockwise. Each play must either play a card higher, or pass.
  • If all players pass, the deck is cleared (player 1 must clear the deck). Whoever played the last card may then starts with any card from their hand.
  • First player to play all cards wins. Play may continue until all hands have been played.

Note: Due to restrictions of the API used to mange the deck, it is up to the players to maintain order. As such, playing in-person is recommended.
